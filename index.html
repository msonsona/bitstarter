FAQ

What is VeTard?
VeTard is a crowdsourcing application where users help each other not to waste time waiting for delayed trains.
Why should I use VeTard?
Do you find yourself stuck at the train station on time, just waiting for your train to arrive? When you left home without having a proper breakfast? Or when you left work knowing you could have used these 10 minutes more productively? Then VeTard is for you!
How does it work?
Being a crowdsourcing app, users provide real-time, location-aware data using their smartphones, which allow the system estimate potential delay or expected arrival time.
Aren't there APIs for that?!
Unluckily not everywhere there is data available about delays and expected arrival times. Or there is the data but it's not publicly accessible.
